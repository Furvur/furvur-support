Privacy Policy

Captionful processes everything locally on your Mac. Your videos, audio, and transcriptions never leave your device.

  • No data collection. We don't collect analytics, usage data, or personal information.
  • No accounts. There's nothing to sign up for.
  • No network requests. Transcription and rendering happen entirely on-device. The only network activity is license validation.
  • No third-party tracking. No SDKs, no telemetry, no ads.

Your files stay on your machine. We never see them.
