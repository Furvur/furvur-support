Privacy Policy

Slim is built with privacy as a core principle. All file compression happens locally on your Mac. Your files never leave your device.

  • No data collection. We do not collect personal information, usage data, analytics, or any identifiers.
  • No accounts. There's nothing to sign up for.
  • No file uploads. Images, videos, audio, and PDFs are compressed entirely on-device using Apple's native frameworks. Nothing is sent to a server.
  • No third-party tracking. No SDKs, no telemetry, no ads. Slim uses only Apple's own frameworks.
  • No network requests. The only network activity is Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchase validation.

Your files stay on your machine. We never see them.

Local Storage

Slim stores a small amount of data locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults:

  • Your compression settings (quality, codec, and format preferences)
  • Your last-used save directory
  • Daily compression count for free tier tracking

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

In-App Purchases

Slim offers a one-time Pro upgrade handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions.

Crash Diagnostics

Slim uses Apple's MetricKit framework for crash diagnostics. This data is sent directly to Apple as part of standard macOS diagnostics, which you control in System Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics. We do not use any third-party crash reporting services.

Temporary Files

During compression, Slim creates temporary files in your system's temporary directory. These are automatically cleaned up when you start a new session or when macOS performs routine cleanup.

Third-Party Services

Slim uses no third-party services or SDKs. It is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks: SwiftUI, AVFoundation, ImageIO, PDFKit, StoreKit, and MetricKit.

Children's Privacy

Slim does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date above. Given that we collect no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
